# E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard

---

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing e-commerce sales data to understand how a business is performing. The main goal is to convert raw data into meaningful insights that can help in better decision-making.
In this project:
- SQL is used to clean and analyze the data  
- Power BI is used to create an interactive dashboard  
- Excel is used for initial data checking  

---

## Objectives
The project is built to solve the following problems:

### Analyze Revenue Trends
Understand how revenue changes over time (monthly).
This helps in identifying:
- Growth patterns  
- Peak sales periods  
- Seasonal trends  

---

### Identify Top and Low-Performing Products
Find which products are:
- Selling the most (high revenue or quantity)  
- Not performing well  
This helps businesses focus on profitable products.

---

### Understand Customer Behavior
Study how customers interact with the business:
- Who are repeat customers?  
- How often do they purchase?  
Helps improve customer retention and marketing strategies.

## Key KPIs (Key Performance Indicators)
KPIs are important metrics used to measure performance.

---

### Total Revenue
Total amount of money generated from all sales.

---

### Total Orders
Total number of orders placed by customers.

---

### Total Customers
Number of unique customers.

---

### Total Products
Number of unique products.

---

### Total Quantity Sold
Total number of items sold.

---

### Repeat Customers
Customers who have made more than one purchase.
Shows customer loyalty.

---

### Order Success Rate
Percentage of successfully delivered orders.
Formula:
Delivered Orders / Total Orders

---

## Dashboard Features

---

### KPI Cards
Displays key metrics at the top of the dashboard for quick understanding.

---

### Revenue Trend Line Chart
Shows how revenue changes over time.
Helps identify growth or decline.

---

### Scatter Plot (Product Performance)
Compares:
- Revenue  
- Quantity sold  
Helps identify:
- High value products  
- Low performing products  

---

### Category Analysis
Shows performance of different categories like:
- Electronics  
- Clothing  
- etc.  
Helps identify best-performing categories.

---

### Interactive Filters (Slicers)
Allows users to filter data based on:
- Date  
- Category  
- Other fields  
Makes dashboard dynamic and user-friendly.
